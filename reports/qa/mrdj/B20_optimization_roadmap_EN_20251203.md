### 12. Risk Management & Contingencies

#### 12.1 Key Risks

**Risk 1: Development Delays**
- **Impact**: HIGH (delays revenue-generating features)
- **Likelihood**: MEDIUM
- **Mitigation**:
  - Hire experienced developer (not junior)
  - Break projects into milestones with buffer time
  - Have backup freelance dev for critical tasks
- **Contingency**: If availability checker delayed, prioritize price calculator (easier to build)

**Risk 2: SEO Content Not Ranking**
- **Impact**: HIGH (SEO strategy fails)
- **Likelihood**: LOW (B10 strategy well-researched)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Keyword research validation before writing
  - Hire SEO-experienced content writer
  - Build backlinks aggressively (don't rely on content alone)
- **Contingency**: Double down on paid ads to compensate for organic shortfall

**Risk 3: A/B Tests Inconclusive**
- **Impact**: MEDIUM (no clear winners, slower CRO progress)
- **Likelihood**: MEDIUM (small sample sizes)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Ensure min. 300 conversions per variant (may take 3-4 weeks)
  - Test big changes (not minor tweaks)
  - Use statistical significance calculator (95% confidence)
- **Contingency**: If test inconclusive, proceed with variant that "feels" better based on qualitative feedback

**Risk 4: Payment Integration Issues**
- **Impact**: CRITICAL (blocks bookings, revenue loss)
- **Likelihood**: LOW (Mollie is reliable)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Thorough testing (50+ test transactions)
  - Sandbox environment before production
  - 24/7 monitoring for payment failures
  - Manual payment fallback (invoice via email)
- **Contingency**: If Mollie down, revert to manual invoicing immediately

**Risk 5: Budget Overrun**
- **Impact**: MEDIUM (delays later initiatives)
- **Likelihood**: MEDIUM (scope creep)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Fixed-price contracts with freelancers
  - Weekly budget reviews
  - Prioritize ruthlessly (drop low-ROI items)
- **Contingency**: Pause Q4 2026 initiatives, focus on ROI-generating Q1-Q3 work
