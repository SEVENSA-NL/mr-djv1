## üÜò Troubleshooting & Tips

### Low Engagement?
- Review posting times (adjust to audience activity)
- Increase CTA clarity
- Add more questions to encourage comments
- Use video content (Reels get 2-3x more reach)

### Low Conversions?
- Simplify CTA (one clear action)
- Add urgency/scarcity language
- Include pricing transparency
- Test different landing pages

### Banner Generation Issues?
- **API Rate Limit**: Wait 10 minutes between batches
- **Poor Quality**: Adjust prompts in content plan JSON
- **Logo Not Visible**: Re-generate with enhanced prompt emphasis
- **Wrong Style**: Update `image_style` field in campaign
