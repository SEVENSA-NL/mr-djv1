## 6. Tone of Voice

### Core Words

**Professional & Warm** (not stiff, not too casual)

- ‚úÖ Professional: Reliable, experienced, structured
- ‚úÖ Warm: Accessible, personal, enthusiastic
- ‚ùå Not: Corporate/stiff, too informal/youthful, aggressive sales

### Do's:

- Use "je" (informal you, not formal "u")
- Be specific with numbers (500+ events, 15+ years)
- Use active language ("Create", "Make", "Guarantee")
- Show personality ("We believe that...")

### Don'ts:

- Don't exaggerate ("the BEST dj ever")

- Not too many emojis (max 1-2 per section)
