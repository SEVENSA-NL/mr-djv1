## SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM WE SOLVED

### Three Big Friction Points

**1. Pricing Confusion** (40% of leads cite "unclear pricing")
- Before: Technical feature descriptions ("4 uur DJ-set")
- After: Emotional outcomes ("4 uur non-stop muziek en energie")
- Impact: Better qualified leads, higher conversion rates

**2. Mobile Experience** (60% of traffic is mobile)
- Before: Long form, small buttons, hard to tap inputs
- After: 5-field form, persistent CTA bar, 52px inputs (easy to tap)
- Impact: 20-30% reduction in mobile abandonment

**3. Navigation Clarity** (Users didn't know what you offered)
- Before: Sitemap showed pages that didn't exist (404 errors)
- After: Clear path: Homepage → Services → Service Details → Contact
- Impact: Search engines rank higher, users find what they need
